BETTER HER THAN ME is one of Boston's most fun high-energy professional bands, featuring great dance music from the 60s through the present.

Motown, R&B, 70s funk, 80s rock, Top 40 pop...you name it, we play it. We specialize in making special events the best they can be, from weddings and family occasions to corporate functions and fundraisers. Members of the band have been playing together for more than two decades, playing hundreds of performances in that time.

Our song list is always expanding and currently includes songs by classic acts like The Beatles, The Temptations, and Stevie Wonder, as well as by modern artists like Katy Perry, Cee Lo Green, Bruno Mars and Pharrell Williams. Don't see the song you're looking for? Just ask!

We know our job is to keep you and your guests entertained and on the dance floor. And that's what we do best.
